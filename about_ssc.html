About Sancta Space Club

The Sancta Space Club is a space where students passionate about aeronautics and Space Science can come together to share findings, have rocket competitions and to hangout with people who share your passion for aeronautics. You can sign up to join the Sancta Space Club in the schoology link and get a badge.
