About Sancta Space Club

The Sancta Space Club is a space where students passionate about aeronautics and space science can come together to share findings, have rocket competitions, and hang out with people who share your passion for aerospace. You can sign up to join the Sancta Space Club through the Schoology link and receive a badge.
